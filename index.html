Ruby DK Conference 2026

A one-day mini conference for the Danish Ruby community

All talks in English
📅 Saturday, November 7, 2026 📍 Merkle, Copenhagen 🕔 10:00 – 17:00

About

Ruby DK Conference is a one-day mini conference bringing together Ruby developers from across Denmark and beyond. Whether you're a seasoned Rubyist or just getting started, join us for a day of talks, learning, and networking with the community.

All talks will be held in English. The full speaker lineup will be announced soon — stay tuned!

Open spaces & networking rooms — Beyond the main talks, additional meeting rooms will be available throughout the day. Use them to have informal discussions, give lightning talks, demo your Ruby projects, or simply meet fellow Rubyists.
